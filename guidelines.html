Guidelines & Rules

How to use STARS and earn merit points

📘 How to Use the STARS Website

1

Register your account

Go to the login page and click Register. Enter your full name, Student ID (format: YYYY-XXXXX), and create a password. You only need to do this once.

2

Log in to your account

Use your Student ID and password to sign in. You will be redirected to your Dashboard automatically.

3

Check your Dashboard

Your Dashboard shows your Total Merit Points and your points per category: Scholar Track, Punctual Pass, Achievers Mark, and Engage Badge.

4

Submit an Achievement

Click Submit Achievement on the sidebar. Fill in the title, pick a category, add a short description, and upload your proof (certificate, photo, or PDF — max 5MB). Then click Submit for Review.

5

Wait for Admin Approval

Your submission will show as Pending while it is being reviewed. The admin will approve or reject it. Points are only added after approval.

6

Redeem your Rewards

Once you have enough points, go to Rewards and click Redeem Now on any unlocked reward. Show the confirmation to your CCS coordinator to claim it.

📌 Important Rules

  • ✅ Submit only legitimate achievements with valid proof.
  • ✅ One submission per achievement only — no duplicate submissions.
  • ✅ Proof must be clear and readable (JPG, PNG, or PDF, max 5MB).
  • ✅ Points are only credited after admin approval.
  • ✅ Lost & Found PIN codes can only be used once.
  • ❌ Do not submit fake or edited certificates — this will result in disqualification.
  • ❌ Redeemed rewards cannot be refunded or exchanged.
  • ❌ Account sharing is not allowed.
